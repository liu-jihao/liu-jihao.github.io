About

I am a researcher at the Department of Mathematics, Peking University.

I was a Boas Assistant Professor at the Department of Mathematics, Northwestern University from September 1st, 2021 to August 31st, 2024, supervised by Professor Yuchen Liu.

I received my Ph.D. in March 2021 from the University of Utah, under the supervision of Professor Christopher D. Hacon FRS. I obtained my Bachelor's degree in July 2015 from Peking University, where my undergraduate advisor was Professor Gang Tian.

Research Interests

Pure mathematics. Algebraic geometry. Birational geometry. Singularities. Foliations. Explicit birational geometry.

Current Activities

I am currently organizing Peking University's Algebraic Geometry Seminar (information also available on Professor Qizheng Yin's webpage or the official webpage) with Fumiaki Suzuki, Zhiyu Tian, and Qizheng Yin.

I am a member of the organization committee (together with Professors Ruochuan Liu, Zhiyu Tian, and Qizheng Yin) of the 5th National Algebraic Geometry Conference.

I am also organizing the Conference on Foliation, Fibration, and Moduli, to be held at Westlake University, Hangzhou, August 24–29, 2026.
