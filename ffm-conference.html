Conference on Foliation, Fibration, and Moduli

The past few years have seen rapid progress in the birational geometry of foliations and fibrations — in particular on boundedness and moduli, and on the way these problems talk to KSBA and K-moduli theory. This conference brings together people working on these threads from different angles, with the hope that a week in Hangzhou produces some new connections (and a few new theorems).
